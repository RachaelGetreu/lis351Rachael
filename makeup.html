Makeup

I love makeup! I am a self taught makeup artist. I started doing my own makeup for my dance competitions when I was 10 years old. Youtube played a big role in learning how to do my makeup and new tecniques.

Here are some of my favorite makeup items

Nars Light Reflecting Advanced Skincare Foundation
This is a lightweight foundation that I apply with a Beauty Blender. I wear shade Santa Fe - M2.
Rare Beauty Soft Pinch Liquid Blush
This is a highly pigmented blush that I like to layer with a powder blush. I wear shade Happy which is a bright pink color.
Tarte Maracuja Juicy Lip Plump
This is a plumping lipgloss. I have it in shades primrose and cherry blossom.
